## Getting Started

### Your Anonymous Identity

When you first visit SafeVoice, you're automatically assigned a unique anonymous ID in the format `Student#XXXX`. This ID:

- **Stays completely private** - No personal information required
- **Persists across sessions** - Your ID is saved locally in your browser
- **Cannot be traced** - We don't track or store identifying information

### Accessing the Feed

Navigate to the Community Feed from:
- **Desktop/Tablet**: Click **"Feed"** in the main navigation bar
- **Mobile**: Tap the **Feed icon** in the bottom navigation bar

The feed displays all community posts sorted by:
1. **Pinned posts** (from moderators)
2. **Newest posts** first

## Creating Content

### Making Your First Post

1. Click the **"Share your story"** button at the top of the feed
2. Write your content (minimum 5 characters)
3. *Optional*: Add a category (e.g., Academic, Mental Health, Campus Life)
4. *Optional*: Attach an image
5. Choose post lifetime (how long it stays visible)
6. Click **"Post"** to publish

### Post Lifetime Options

Control how long your post remains visible:

| Lifetime | Duration | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **1 Hour** | 60 minutes | Quick questions |
| **6 Hours** | 6 hours | Time-sensitive topics |
| **24 Hours** | 1 day | Daily discussions |
| **7 Days** | 1 week | Weekly topics |
| **30 Days** | 1 month | Long-term discussions |
| **Never** | Permanent | Important resources |
| **Custom** | Your choice | Specific needs |

### First Post Bonus üéâ

Your very first post earns you **20 VOICE tokens** - double the normal reward! This welcome bonus helps you get started in the community.

### Post Categories

Choose from:
- **Academic** - Study tips, exam help, course discussions
- **Mental Health** - Emotional support, wellness, counseling
- **Campus Life** - Events, activities, general campus talk
- **Relationships** - Friendships, dating, social connections
- **Career** - Internships, placements, job opportunities
- **Other** - Everything else

### Crisis Detection üö®

SafeVoice automatically detects posts with crisis keywords (self-harm, suicide, abuse) and:
- Shows you immediate support resources
- Flags the post for priority community support
- Awards **100 VOICE tokens** to helpful responders
- Connects you to verified 24/7 helplines

## Privacy & Safety

### Your Data

- **No account required** - Browse and post anonymously
- **Local storage only** - Data stored in your browser
- **No tracking** - We don't collect personal information
- **No IP logging** - Your location stays private

### Anonymous Wallet (Optional)

Create an encrypted wallet to:
- Back up your VOICE tokens
- Transfer tokens between devices
- Participate in on-chain governance

**To create:**
1. Go to your Wallet section
2. Click "Generate Anonymous Wallet"
3. Set a strong password
4. **Save your recovery phrase** (12-24 words)
5. Never share your password or recovery phrase

### Content Moderation

- Posts are scanned for crisis keywords
- Sensitive content may be blurred
- Violations may be hidden or removed
- Moderators review flagged content

### Safety Tips

- üõ°Ô∏è Never share personal information (name, phone, address)
- üõ°Ô∏è Don't arrange in-person meetups
